== Description ==


= What is Autorole? = 

Autorole is a Discord role management plugin that automates and streamlines subscription management so you can focus on improving your offerings. This automated software is easily integrated into both WooCommerce and WordPress websites with just a few clicks - no coding required! Say goodbye to manually handling each new subscription and hello to hands-free processing. 

= Improve Subscription Management & Free Up Valuable Time = 
Running a Discord server is a full-time job that requires a lot of financial and time investment. Unfortunately, many server hosts are bogged down by the everyday tasks of managing individual subscriptions which greatly limits their growth and profits. That’s where Autorole comes into play. This advanced and highly effective Discord subscription plug-in automates the whole process of onboarding new subscribers. This allows you to focus on actually growing your audience, improving your offerings, and increasing your profits.

= Why choose Autorole? = 
**WordPress & WooCommerce Compatible**
Autorole is specifically designed to function smoothly with WordPress and WooCommerce sites, which are the two of the most popular web platforms for Discord server hosts. Within just a few clicks you’ll have everything you need to get started.

**Complete Automation**
This Discord role management subscription plugin is 100% automated. Autrole automatically handles sign-ups, upgrades, downgrades, and cancellations by assigning the appropriate permissions to users. You don’t have to lift a finger. 

**Insightful Analytics**
Autrole provides Discord server hosts with a wealth of information about their server performance. With an easy-to-use and dynamic dashboard, you’ll have the power to make bulk modifications, add roles, track profits, and so much more. 

= What Does Autorole Do? =
- **Discord Role Management** Automate the management of subscriber roles. 
- **Subscription Analytics** Get accurate and real-time analytics on your server’s performance. 
- **Bulk Subscription Management** Make bulk modifications to subscriptions with just a few clicks. 
- **Email Notifications** Stay in touch with new subscribers automatically. 
- **Direct Support** Get round-the-clock support directly through Discord. 

== Installation ==

1. Install Autorole Plugin
1. Visit app.autorole.io to confirm plugin linkage.
1. Follow steps on app.autorole.io. No other actions are required in WordPress.
